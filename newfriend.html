Avocarlos meets Sirtet

Avocarlos walked past the concrete landing of the library, and instead walked into a nearby field.

Interestingly, there was a small blue block on the ground, inexplicably in the center of the grassy landscape.

Curious, Avocarlos approached it.

"Why, hello there!", the small block on the ground cheerfully exclaimed.

Avocarlos was obviously taken aback by this. After all, everyone knows Avocados can talk, but not square blocks!

"Oh! I suppose you're surpised to see me.", the block added after Avocarlos had fallen to the ground in surprise.

"My name is Sirtet! By chance, do you happen to know Kiaan?"

Avocarlos cheerfully nodded. Kiaan was the artist responsible for the creation of Avocarlos, of course.

"So, you know the vile Java has captured all of his free time, and endangered the mere concept of fun!"

Avocarlos grimmly nodded. The reason he had begun his new quest was his concern at Kiaan and his many friends starting a second term at BCIT.

Java's ultimate scheme is to make that experience somewhat less fun. How Awful!

"I Sirtet, will help you in your quest, Avocarlos! Let's head to the school library, and I will explain the plan to rescue the world from Java!"

Sirtet joined Avocarlos' party!
