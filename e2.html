Elderberry Path - C's Trap

Rhys and Avocarlos were pretty proud of themselves for trying some computer skills.

"Yo! Java! You're really dumb!" Rhys cockily snapped out towards an innocent clump of flowers.

"Yo! Rhys! You're even dumber!" The flowers yelled back.

Wait? The flowers yelled back! What?!

"Thanks to me!" An unknown voice shot out, which also was a rather scary voice.

"W-w-w-who's that?" Rhys said in wavering form.

"Hello! Haven't met me yet, have you? I am C!"

"To be frank, I'll be around a lot for your second term of CST."

"I'll be helping Java carry out his plans."

"What does that have to do with talking flowers?" Rhys, sarcasm returning, sputtered out.

"Everything, as you'll soon find out!" C flashed a sinister smile. "Now my flower army! Show these two the pain and power of procedual pollen!"

The flowers began spraying clouds of pollen in the air without remorse. Rhys and Avocarlos stumbled around in the confusion, being unable to do much of anything.
