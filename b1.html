Blueberry Neighbourhood - Refreshing Path

Avocarlos left the university campus, and was happy to have discovered the wonderful scenery surrounding the area.

Before long, he encountered someone else. A short, round, yet cheerful fruit sat on a bench working on their own laptop.

However, her happiness did not last terribly long. Suddenly, she turned pale. "I forgot the images for my webpage!" she gasped aloud.

"My assignment is almost due! How could I possibly find them in time?!"

Avocarlos blinked, and it came to him. This must be another student from BCIT.

She needed help, quickly. Avocarlos looked around. There was beautiful scenery all around the pair.

"Excuse me." Avocarlos politely said. "What do you need for your project?"

"Images for a project on the neighbourhood around campus, ironically! There just isn't enough time to take my own pictures or head back to campus to download some."

"In fact, in a few minutes I'll have to head back to submit this for partial marks..." She said with a heavy sigh.

Avocarlos just had to do something.
