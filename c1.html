Cantelope Labs - Glass Tunnel

Uh oh. Avocarlos and Sirtet were LOST. The Cantelope Labs in the school were surrounded by an extensive glass hallway, one that was a little difficult to naviagate.

No matter how far they would walk, they would end up passing by the same doorways and staircases without fail.

Worse yet, these landmarks were not useful in locating anyone who needed help from Avocarlos. They were simply more dead ends.

"We're going to walk in circles forever..." Sirtet moaned.

The blocky character floated to the ground in exhaustion, much to Avocarlos' dismay. How could Sirtet possibly complain about walking when he could float everywhere and anywhere?

Avocarlos did have worry building up inside himself, however. Java could be up to no shortage of diabolical plots involving sinister mutators and unexpected accessors.

Night had fallen, and all Avocarlos could see in the glass was his perplexed reflection.
