The Story of Avocarlos

Avocarlos quickly scaled the stairs at the front of the BCIT library, as Sirtet magically floated inside after him.

Avocarlos was happy to see the library was open, but he didn't see a single student he could recognize from the CST program.

"I must conclude there is an important lecture for the CST students at this time, Avocarlos." Sirtet explained as the Avocado looked around the broad entranceway.

Avocarlos still seemed a little gloomy, so Sirtet continued to talk:

"I guess you must be wondering where I came from, right?"

Avocarlos turned to face Sirtet. Despite concern for the CST students being bored by the troublemaker Java, he was curious to learn more about his new hovering cuboid pal.

"I was destined to fill the gap in a line of Tetris, which Kiaan was playing during one lecture in the past."

"Yet Kiaan had to stop playing so he could finish a Java lab, and my destiny was ruined..."

"That's why we have to help the students here! So Java won't overtake their entire life!"

Avocarlos appreciated the bravery of Sirtet, and thus decided to continue with the quest.
