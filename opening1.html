Avocarlos Arrives

As a cool winter breeze lingered in the air, Avocarlos stepped off the bus taking him to BCIT.

The grand building in front of Avocarlos served as entrance to the rest of campus, glass panels glistening many colours in the faint sunlight.

He walked past this, and continued through the sprawling world in front of him until he reached the heart of the school, and the library was in sight.

Avocarlos reflected upon his goal: To free the students of BCIT from the clutches of Java.

Something pulled him out of his thoughts, however. Something in the grassy fields bordering the parking lots tugged at his attention, yet he could not determine why.
